`steam:` Protocol
-----------------

### Launch Steam

* [`steam://''`](steam://'') - Launch Steam
* [`steam://'-dev'`](steam://'-dev') - Launch Steam in developer mode
* [`steam://'-console'`](steam://'-console') - Launch Steam with console (enabled but hidden)
