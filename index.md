`steam:` Protocol
-----------------

### Launch Steam

* <a href="steam://''">`steam://''`</a> - Launch Steam
* <a href="steam://'-dev'">`steam://'-dev'`</a> - Launch Steam in developer mode
* <a href="steam://'-console'">`steam://'-console'`</a> - Launch Steam with console (enabled but hidden)
